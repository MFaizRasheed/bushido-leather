Good to know

Delivery & Returns

Delivery

We deliver all across Pakistan via courier. Orders are dispatched within 1–2 working days and typically arrive in 3–5 working days. Payment is cash on delivery — pay only when your order is in your hands.

Cancellation & Returns

Cancel free of charge any time before dispatch — just message us on the same WhatsApp number where you placed your order. Received a damaged or wrong item? Contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo and we'll exchange it or refund you. Items must be unused and in original condition. Shoe size exchanges are welcome.

About Bushido Leather

Bushido Leather is a Pakistani house of fine timepieces, genuine leather wallets, and handcrafted shoes. Every piece is personally selected and photographed by us — what you see is exactly what you receive.
